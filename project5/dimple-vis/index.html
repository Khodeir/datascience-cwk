Sales and stock over time

This graph presents the monthly totals of sales and warehouse stock of an anonymous mid-sized trading company. The company operated a single warehouse until August 2015, when a second warehouse more than doubled its capacity.

Despite this extra capacity, sales remain unchanged, with a ceiling or upper bound of 1,200 tons per month. This translates into an increase in working capital without a corresponding increase in revenue.

In the coming months, the company intends to become focused on bolstering its sales effort, with emphasis on customer aquisition.
